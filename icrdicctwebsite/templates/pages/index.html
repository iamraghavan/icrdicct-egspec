ABOUT THE COLLEGE

E. G. S. Pillay Engineering College

E. G. S. Pillay Engineering College is one of the pioneering non-grant Autonomous Engineering Colleges in the State. Established in 1995 by the G. S. Pillay & Sons Educational & Charitable Trust, Nagapattinam, the college received sanction from the Government of Tamil Nadu and approval from the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. Since 2002, it has been affiliated with Anna University, Chennai, awarding degrees as per Government Orders.

Accredited by NAAC with A++ grade and NBA (Tier-1) for B.Tech-IT, B.E-CSE & ECE, the college is renowned for its excellent infrastructure and facilities conducive to learning. Over the years, it has consistently delivered impressive performance, earning the trust of students and parents alike. The college is committed to empowering youth aspiring to excel as Engineers, Scientists, and Managers through expanded programs and enriching activities.

For more information, visit egspec.org.

Nagapattinam

Nagapattinam: Situated in the coastal region of Tamil Nadu, Nagapattinam is a historically significant town known for its rich cultural heritage. The town boasts of a vibrant community with a blend of traditional values and modern outlook.

Climate: Nagapattinam experiences a tropical climate, characterized by hot summers and moderate winters. The coastal location influences the weather patterns, making it pleasant for most of the year.

Language: Tamil is predominantly spoken in Nagapattinam, reflecting the cultural and linguistic heritage of the region. However, due to its historical significance and diverse population, you may also encounter people fluent in other languages.

Transportation: Nagapattinam is well-connected by road and rail networks, facilitating easy access to nearby cities and towns. The town also has a bustling port, playing a significant role in trade and commerce.

This conference aims to address the research challenges and development in the field of information, communication and computing technology.
